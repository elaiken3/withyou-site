Inside the app

  • Capture thoughts fast when you’re overwhelmed

Built for adults with diagnosed or self-identified ADHD.

How it helps

Start smaller than you think

When your brain freezes, “do the whole thing” isn’t a plan. With You centers the first tiny move.

Reduce emotional friction

It’s not about motivation. It’s about the feeling of starting. The app is designed to be calm and non-judgmental.

Support without pressure

No streaks, no punishment for stopping, no “you failed” vibes. Just a steady hand when you’re stuck.

What it is (and what it isn’t)

It is

  • A gentle tool for task initiation
  • A calm place to capture and sort thoughts
  • Support for overwhelm and getting unstuck

It isn’t

  • A “grind harder” productivity system
  • A streak-based habit tracker
  • A replacement for therapy or medical care

With You is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice. If you’re in crisis or feel unsafe, contact local emergency services or a trusted support line.
